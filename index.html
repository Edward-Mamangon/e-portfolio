About Me

My name is Edward Mamangon. I'm a Graphic Designer / Frontend Developer based in the Philippines. In 2008 I worked as a Graphic Designer for 3 years in a private enterprise specializing in importation of auto parts, accessories, and lubricants. In 2012 I started my Freelancing career as a Graphic Designer until 2021.

I'm currently attending a Web Development bootcamp at KodeGo. The course covers both frontEnd and backend development.
